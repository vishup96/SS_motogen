About Us

SS Motogen Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in March 2017.

 

We are an Automobile Sales and Service Company associated with TATA Motors as it’s Authorized Dealer for Personal Vehicles. We are presently operational in Ranchi, Dhanbad, Giridih, Deoghar, Koderma and Lohardagga districts of Jharkhand.
